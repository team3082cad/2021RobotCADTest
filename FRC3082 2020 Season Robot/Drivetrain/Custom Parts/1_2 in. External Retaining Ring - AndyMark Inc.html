Product Overview

Retaining rings are used to hold components onto a shaft when installed. The ring is opened using Retaining Ring Pliers (am-4013) and released into a groove on a shaft. The retaining ring then prevents components from moving past it. This retaining ring fits on a groove for 1/2 inch diameter shafts. You can find this retaining ring in our RAW Box gearbox.

Specifications

  • Free Inside Diameter: 0.461 in.
  • Groove Diameter: 0.468 in.
  • Groove Width: 0.039 in.
  • Material: Steel
  • Shaft Diameter: 1/2 in.
  • Thickness: 0.035 in.
  • Weight: 0.002 Lbs
